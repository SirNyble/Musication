The shape of a note shows how many beats it has in a bar.

Whole Note

A whole note will take up all of the beats in a bar.

Half Note

A half note will take up half of beats of a whole note.

Quarter Note

A quarter note will take up half of the beats of a half note.

Eighth Note

An eighth note will take up half of the beats of a quarter note.

Sixteenth Note

A sixteenth note will take up half of the beats of an eighth note.
