    Woodwinds
  • These instruments use a thin piece of wood called a reed, which vibrates when you blow through it.
  • The flute is a woodwind instrument, but does not use a reed. It is still played by blowing into it.
  • Includes:
  • Flute
  • Clarinet
  • Saxophone
  • Bagpipes
