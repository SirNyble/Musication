Woodwinds
  • These instruments use a thin piece of wood called a reed, which vibrates when you blow through it.
  • The flute is a woodwind instrument, but does not use a reed. It is still played by blowing into it.
Brass
  • These instruments have mouth pieces that you vibrate your lips into to make sound.
  • Some brass instruments have keys that you press (trumpet), while others have pieces that you move (trombone).
String
  • These instruments have strings that you vibrate to make sound.
  • Some are played by plucking the strings, while others slide the strings with a bow.
Percussion
  • These instruments are played by hitting them with a stick or your hands, or shaking.
Keyboards
  • These instruments have a set of keys that sound off notes when pressed.
  • Keyboards may hit strings, blow air, or use electricity to play sounds.
